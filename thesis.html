Abstract · 摘要 Hover highlighted terms to explore chapters

隨著生成式人工智慧（Generative AI）Chapter 01研究背景與動機的迅速崛起，其應用在建築設計領域仍面臨流程斷裂與知識整合的挑戰。現有AI工具多為分散式應用，難以觸及設計過程中複雜、隱性的決策網絡。為此，本研究的核心目標是建構一個名為CoEvo的生成式AI多代理（Multi-Agent）協作系統Chapter 02文獻回顧，旨在將建築設計中隱性的知識與複雜的流程，正規化為一個由多AI代理協同工作的可追蹤、可編輯的節點工作流程。如此AI才能更緊密的與人類協作，有效率地生成出具有建築內涵的設計方案。 With the rapid rise of Generative AIChapter 01Background & Motivation, its application in architecture faces challenges of process fragmentation and knowledge integration. Existing AI tools are mostly decentralized, making it hard to reach the complex, implicit decision networks in design. This research introduces CoEvo, a Multi-Agent collaborative systemChapter 02Literature Review, aimed at formalizing implicit design knowledge and workflows into a traceable, editable node-based process. This integration enables AI to collaborate more deeply with humans, efficiently generating designs with architectural depth.

本研究以人類的設計思考流程為基礎，探索如何將生成式人工智慧與多代理系統，整合應用於建築設計的創新流程Chapter 03整合應用於建築設計的創新流程，提煉出兩種整合人類設計思考與人工智慧的設計探索模式：「目標導向的最佳化設計流程」與「廣域探索導向的同步化設計流程」。接著，為支持這兩種模式，本研究設計、實現並比較了兩種對應的多智能體協作架構：一種是強調穩定與效率的順序化協作架構Chapter 03 · Architecture A順序化協作架構，適用於對單一方案的深度精煉；另一種是具備高度彈性與動態性的層級化協作架構Chapter 03 · Architecture B層級化協作架構，適用於設計初期的多路徑並行探索。 Grounded in human design thinking, this study explores integrating GenAI and multi-agent systemsChapter 03Integrated Innovation Process into architectural design, distilling two exploration patterns: "Goal-oriented optimization" and "Broad exploration-oriented synchronization." To support these, two architectures are implemented and compared: a Sequential collaboration architectureChapter 03 · Architecture ASequential Architecture for deep refinement, and a highly flexible Hierarchical collaboration architectureChapter 03 · Architecture BHierarchical Architecture for early multi-path exploration.

通過在CoEvo平台上的案例實證與比較分析Chapter 04案例實證與比較分析，研究結果表明，層級化協作架構不僅在支持複雜的廣域探索時表現出顯著優勢，其靈活性更使其能夠向下兼容並模擬順序化的優化流程，展現出作為未來通用型設計平台的巨大潛力；而順序化架構則在高度標準化的特定任務中，仍保有其效率價值。 Through case studies and comparative analysisChapter 04Case Studies & Analysis on CoEvo, results show the Hierarchical architecture excels in complex exploration and remains versatile enough to simulate sequential optimization, showing potential as a universal design platform. The sequential architecture maintains efficiency for standardized tasks.

本研究的核心貢獻不僅在於提供了一個可行的系統框架，更在於提出了一套以多代理系統為基礎的設計流程方法論Chapter 05結論與未來展望，並揭示了多AI協作對建築師創意與多樣化探索策略的影響。CoEvo的實踐，為未來開發能與建築師深度協同、重塑建築師價值核心的AI輔助設計代理，提供了關鍵的理論基礎與實踐洞見，期望能藉此推動建築設計流程的協作效率與創新潛力。 Beyond a framework, this study proposes a multi-agent design methodologyChapter 05Conclusion & Future Work, revealing AI's impact on architectural creativity. CoEvo provides key theoretical and practical insights for future AI agents that deeply integrate with architects, aiming to enhance collaborative efficiency and innovation in design workflows.
