01 · Healthcare Architecture · 2022

森源重生Morigaki Rebirth

亞洲建築新人戰：佳作Asian Architecture Newcomer Competition: Excellent Work

講述小建築如何在疫情時代下成為緩兵之計，透過將可組合的多變隔離小單元導入基地，解決醫療量能的同時也活化了荒廢數十年但富有歷史韻味的都市孔隙。 Morigaki Rebirth explores how small buildings became a buffer during the pandemic. By integrating modular, adaptable isolation units into the base, they addressed medical capacity issues while revitalizing urban spaces rich in history that had been abandoned for decades.

當疫情緩解後，場所的記憶正式發酵，活動得以進入基地，而單元則功成身退，有機的結合古蹟使場所重生。 Once the pandemic eased, the site's memory began to flourish, allowing activities to flow into the base while the units were retired. This organic integration with the historic site led to its rebirth.

Modular Adaptive Reuse Post-Pandemic
02 · Urban Regeneration · 2021

Stay by Green Waterway

綠川托老托幼兼青創中心Green Waterway Daycare & Innovation Center

私心將從小喜歡的美式漫畫風格帶入版面，成為一個有趣的圖面表達方式。這個作品講述在台中綠川旁的一棟廢棄大樓，如何搖身變為迎接綠川的口袋公園，同時綠意盎然、機能完善、承襲舊有文化特色造型的托老托幼兼青創中心。 Stay by Green Waterway is a unique work that incorporates the American comic book style I've loved since childhood into its layout, creating a playful visual expression. This work tells the story of how an abandoned building beside the Green River in Taichung was transformed into a pocket park that welcomes the Green River. It also serves as a daycare center for the elderly, children, and youth entrepreneurs, embodying the unique cultural heritage of the past.

透過主角威廉的帶領，展開遊歷這棟建築的冒險！ Led by the protagonist William, we embark on an adventure through this building!

03 · Ecological Intervention · 2022

Acupuncture for Aqenna

American Master Prize：Best of Best · 2Winner

基隆正濱漁港Zhengbin Port, Keelung

基地位於基隆正濱漁港一處富有歷史意義的造船廠廢墟遺址，正面臨著極端氣候、生態危機，以及疫情的影響。本設計巧妙地用一個手法同時解決數個問題——在修建計畫的前提下挖開基地，使潮汐得以進入，讓再利用的浮動板控制疫情下的動線，發展觀光的可能性，同時將沃土和魚苗緩慢復育。 Acupuncture for Aqenna is located at a historically significant shipyard ruin in Keelung's Zhengbin Port, facing extreme climate, ecological crises, and the pandemic. We cleverly solved multiple problems at once: excavating the site to allow tidal entry, using reused floating platforms to control circulation during the pandemic, developing tourism possibilities, and slowly restoring soil and fish fry.

American Master Prize Tidal Design Ecology

04 · Smart Infrastructure · 2021

破風之針Acupuncture for Wind

第十五屆創意未來智慧化建築設計創意競賽：銀獎15th Creative Future Smart Building Design: Silver Award中技社科技創意獎CTCI Technology Innovation Award

面對大樓風對台北多處公園的活動產生影響，破風之針作為一種智慧的風力發電單元，同時具備展開扇葉及牆幕的功能，改善都市微氣候及熱島效應的同時，為市民帶來更豐富的空置地使用可能性。 In response to high-rise winds affecting activities in Taipei parks, the Wind Needle, a smart wind turbine, can deploy both blades and walls. It improves urban microclimate and heat island effect while providing more possibilities for vacant land use.

05 · Resilience Design · 2022

城市 T 細胞Urban T Cells

做為一個海平面上升及災後對策的幻想提案，意圖使簡易的多功能 T 細胞單元能夠組成應付各式需求的模矩。平時常駐於都市孔隙之中多功能使用活化空間。 Urban T Cells, a visionary proposal for sea level rise and post-disaster mitigation, aims to transform simple, multifunctional units into modules for diverse needs, revitalizing urban voids.
