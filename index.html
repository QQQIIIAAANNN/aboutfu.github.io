01 // ABOUT關於我

HELLO, I AM FU.你好，我是傅。

我是傅乙晟，畢業於建築系，隨後在建築研究所數位組深入研究人工智慧與建築設計的結合。

秉持著對建築的初衷與熱情，我不斷嘗試以更高效率、更多元突破的方式來探索設計的可能性。我的作品不僅涵蓋傳統建築設計，還延伸到跨領域的創新實踐，特別是將 AI 技術應用於建築流程與設計思維之中。

這些嘗試不只是技術上的整合，更是對未來建築創作模式與空間體驗的一種探索與挑戰。如果你對「AI×建築設計」的可能性感興趣，歡迎與我聯繫，一起探討更多跨域的創意合作。

My name is Fu Yi-Cheng. I graduated from the Department of Architecture and subsequently pursued a career in the Digital Design Group at the Institute of Architecture, focusing on the integration of artificial intelligence and architectural design.

Maintaining my original passion for architecture, I continually strive to explore the possibilities of design through more efficient, diverse, and groundbreaking methods. My work encompasses not only traditional architectural design but also crosses over into innovative cross-disciplinary practices, particularly the application of AI technology to architectural processes and design thinking.

These attempts are not merely technological integrations; they are explorations and challenges into future architectural creative models and innovative spatial experiences. If you are interested in the possibilities of "AI × Architectural Design" or would like to explore more cross-disciplinary creative collaborations, please feel free to contact me.
